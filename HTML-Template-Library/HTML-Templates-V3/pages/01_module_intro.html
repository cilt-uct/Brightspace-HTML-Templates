 Learning Objectives

When writing learning objectives for a course or module, remember:

  1. Each objective should use a verb that describes a measurable action or behavior
  2. Include the specific circumstances under which the learner is to perform the targeted action or behavior
  3. Keep your learning objectives focused and concise
  4. Ensure your objects are realistic and target the appropriate level of Bloom’s taxonomy
